Privacify is a privacy control center that monitors app permissions, tracks sensor access, and secures your device — all without sending data anywhere.

CORE FEATURES:
• Privacy Dashboard — real-time privacy score with sensor usage overview
• Permission Scanner — identifies apps with risky permission combinations
• App Monitoring — tracks camera, microphone, and location access across apps
• Permission Analytics — breakdowns by permission type and risk level
• Quick Settings Tile — one-tap lockdown from the notification shade
• Home Screen Widget — lockdown toggle with live status indicator
• Lockdown Mode — instant system-wide sensor deactivation

ADVANCED MODE (Root/Shizuku):
• Hardware Kill Switches — disable mic and camera at the system level
• AppOps Control — fine-grained permission management per app
• Auto-Guard — automatically pauses kill switches when sensors are in use

No ads. No tracking. No network access required. All data stays on your device.

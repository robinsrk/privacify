Privacify is a production-grade privacy utility for Android that provides users with a complete control center for managing app permissions and background behavior.

Core Features:

*   **Privacy Dashboard**: Real-time insights into your device's privacy state.
*   **Permission Risk Scanner**: Identify apps with sensitive or risky permission access.
*   **App Behavior Monitoring**: Track camera, microphone, and location usage.
*   **Advanced Mode (Root)**:
    *   Revoke permissions via AppOps.
    *   Freeze/Disable apps.
    *   Hardware Kill Switches (Camera/Microphone).
    *   Lockdown Mode.

Privacify works in both Standard mode for all users and Advanced mode for users with root access, offering unparalleled control over your digital footprint.

Privacify is a privacy control center that helps you monitor and manage app permissions and sensor access on your Android device.

CORE FEATURES:
- Privacy Dashboard with real-time privacy score and app risk overview
- Permission Scanner to identify apps with risky permission combinations
- App Behavior Monitoring for camera, microphone, and location access
- Permission Analytics with breakdowns by type and risk level
- Quick Settings Tile for one-tap lockdown toggle
- Home Screen Widget for lockdown toggle with live status
- System-level Lockdown Mode for instant privacy enforcement

ADVANCED MODE (Root/Shizuku):
- Hardware Kill Switches: Disable microphone and camera at the system level
- AppOps Permission Management: Granular control over individual app permissions
- Auto-Guard: Intelligent automation that temporarily unblocks sensors when actively used

All data stays on your device. No ads, no tracking, no network access required.
